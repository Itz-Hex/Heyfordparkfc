MATCH DAY TIPS

First time visiting?

A few small things that make a big difference for a new family.

Arrive 20 minutes early
Parking fills up fast on Saturday mornings — leave time to walk to the pitch.
Check the weather
Both venues have natural-grass pitches that can get muddy. Spare boots and a towel are your friends.

HOUSE RULES

A few ground rules.

Pitch closures
Decisions on weather-related closures are made by 8am on match days and posted on our Facebook page.
Spectator areas
Please stay off the playing area and respect the marked spectator zones — especially for younger age groups.
Dogs & kit bags
Dogs welcome on leads, but not in the changing rooms. Players' kit bags should be kept off the touchline.
